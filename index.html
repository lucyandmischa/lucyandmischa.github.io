On the day


We're super excited that you're going to be at our wedding, and help us celebrate in style.

The ceremony and celebrations will all be at Sunbeam Studios, on Friday 2nd October 2015.

Please arrive at 3pm sharp.

If you'd like to meet up beforehand, head to Paradise which is a 10 minute walk away. Please speak to Paul Clerkin about this.

The party will go on until midnight. For taxis, speak to the door staff on the day and they will be happy to arrange one for you.

Dress code: dressing colourfully in encouraged.

Accomodation and getting around.

The full address for Sunbeam Studios is Ladbroke Hall, 79 Barlby Rd, London W10 6AZ.

Nearest tube stations are Ladbroke Gove (Circle line and Hammersmith & City line) or Kensal Green (Bakerloo line and London Overground).

For help with your route see TFL Journey Planner.

Here are some Hotels nearby:

  • Portobello House
  • K-West
  • Bayswater House
  • Abbey Court
